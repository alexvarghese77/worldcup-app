How to play

Match prediction

  • Home screen will have matches for current day displayed.
  • Click on each match to predict the goal.
  • Goal limit is 35.
  • Goal predicted can be changed until the timer freezes.
  • Predictions are on daily basis.
  • Every correct goal prediction will be awarded +10 points.
  • The points gained will be carried over to the world cup finals.
  • Each match will have two prediction winners.
  • In case of more than two correct entries, two winners will be selected on lot basis.
  • An entry will not be considered, if match winner predicted is correct, but the goals predicted are wrong.

Mega Challenges

  • Five winners will be selected on lot basis for below predictions:
    • Champioship
    • Runner Up
    • Golden Ball
    • Golden Boot
    • Golden Ball
  • The mega challenge freezes at end of first game week.
